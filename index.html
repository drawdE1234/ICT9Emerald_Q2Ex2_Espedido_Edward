Unique FPS Games

OVERWATCH

Overwatch is a fast-paced FPS made by the developers of CALL of DUTY, ACTIVISION; BLIZZARD, wherein you are required to do certain objectives such as excorting a turck or capturing an area while the enemy team tries to stop you from doing so. It currently has 39 playable characters to play from with different roles that players choos from to get the best synergy to outlast the other.



VALORANT

VALORANT is a tactical FPS made by Riot Games, the developers of League of Legends, wherein an attacking team must plant a "spike" and wait until it explodes while the defenders are required to eliminate the attackers or defuse the spike to win. It currently has 23 playable characters to choose from with different roles that categorizes them from others agents to fit certain playstyles.
